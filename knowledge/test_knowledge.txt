Company Data Policy - Internal Document

Data Cleaning Standards:
All missing values in numerical columns must be filled using the median strategy.
Categorical columns with missing values should use mode imputation.
Duplicate rows must always be removed before any analysis begins.
Columns with more than 40 percent missing values should be dropped entirely.

Customer Segmentation Rules:
Customers are segmented into three tiers: Gold, Silver, and Bronze.
Gold customers have made more than 10 purchases in the last 6 months.
Silver customers have made between 5 and 10 purchases in the last 6 months.
Bronze customers have made fewer than 5 purchases in the last 6 months.

Sales Performance Metrics:
Monthly revenue targets are set at the start of each quarter.
A sales rep is considered high performing if they exceed their target by 20 percent.
Underperforming reps are those who fall below 80 percent of their monthly target.
Regional performance is reviewed every quarter by the analytics team.

Churn Risk Indicators:
A customer is flagged as high churn risk if they have not made a purchase in 90 days.
Customers who have raised more than 3 support tickets in a month are also flagged.
Churn risk scores are recalculated every week using the latest transaction data.
The retention team must be notified within 24 hours of a high risk flag.
